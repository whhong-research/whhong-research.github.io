海上风机机会性运维优化

Oct 27, 2025

风机运维文献整理

Li, M., Jiang, X., Carroll, J., & Negenborn, R. R. (2022). A multi-objective maintenance strategy optimization framework for offshore wind farms considering uncertainty. Applied Energy, 321, 119284. https://doi.org/10.1016/j.apenergy.2022.119284

Introduction

【运维成本占全生命周期成本的14%-30%】

”Operation and Maintenance (O&M) costs have been estimated to occupy around 14%–30% of life cycle cost (LCC) “

【海上风机不可用性显著下降至60%-70%】

once wind turbines are placed offshore instead of onshore, the availability may decrease significantly to as low as 60%–70%

Literature Review

【运维策略】【纠正性运维】【预防性运维】【机会性运维】

  • Corrective maintenance: carried out only after turbines break down.
  • Preventive maintenance: conducted at specific intervals, known as periodic maintenance.
  • Corrective maintenance and preventive maintenance are still the dominated maintenance strategies applied in wind energy industry.
  • Performing maintenance on one component generates an opportunity window which can be taken advantage of to repair other components qualified for maintenance actions in this wind farm.
  • [决策变量与部件的可靠度或平均失效时间相关] The decision variables in the opportunistic maintenance model are mostly set as maintenance thresholds related to component reliability or mean time to failure.
  • **[Gap 1]: ** These models did not connect the maintenance threshold with component condition.
    • Condition monitoring technique 发展用于采集诊断信号（能够反映关键部件的结构状态）
    • 剩余寿命预测模型辅助决策者根据结构未来状态进行预测性运维

【不确定性】

  • 全生命周期运维框架中存在诸多不确定性：statistical uncertainty of component reliability estimations, inaccessibility of maintenance affected by weather variety, insufficient maintenance sources to support maintenance.
  • [Gap 2] : 【涉及的不确定性因素少】【未讨论策略优化问题】【仅考虑传统运维策略】
    • Only study the impact of one or two types of uncertainty on the maintenance performance.
    • Not involving optimization.
    • Novel maintenance strategies are not considered.
    • 没有展现不确定性对maintenance性能的影响
  • 文中主要考虑的不确定性包括：故障时间的随机性，真实故障时间与实际故障时间的偏差；不确定性维护后果；
  • 未考虑的不确定性因素包括：天气因素影响的可达性，船舶以及备件的可用性等；

【Maintenance models包括哪些部分】

  • In general, the maintenance models cover the aspects including the modelling of the deterioration of the system, the description of the available information about the system state, the possible actions and consequences
  • 退化模型+系统当前状态的可用信息+可能采取的运维动作+运维后果

【运维目标】

  • 运维目标可能包括：reliability, safety, life-cycle cost, carbon emission等
  • 本文考虑的运维目标：
    • minumum costs related to maintenance effort. 最小化维护费用
    • minumum production losses caused by downtime. 最小化能源损失

Maintenance model

【Assumption】

  • 所有风机属于同一类型，简化为多个关键组件的集合，任何组件的损坏会导致整个系统的失效
  • 做出运维决策后，维护活动能顺利实施
  • 不考虑材料、船舶租赁以及人力相关成本的时间波动

【Failure Modelling】

  • 使用双参数的威布尔分布来模拟失效概率的密度函数：

  • 除了退化之外（degradation），还考虑了随机破坏（意外情况，例如雷击、飓风等），这种影响被建立为非均匀泊松过程。给定一个强度函数λ，利用逆分布得到撞击发生的时间；

  • 环境影响被分为三个决策状态：Critical, influential, and minor

    • The component subject to critical impact directly breaks down.
    • The influential impact has an effect on the degradation of components, causing an abrupt increase of age b_m;
    • The effect of minor impact is relatively small, only affecting the operation of turbine temporarily. Then, the turbine recovers to normal operational state after a short period of time.

【Maintenance opportunities and component condition】

  • 执行maintenance的机会：

    • Failure-based opportunity: 风机中的一个组件由于degradation failure发生后造成wind turbine停机;
    • Incident-based opportunity: 风机遭受了sudden critical incideng;
    • Ageing-based opportunity: No failure occurs in the farm, but a certain percentage of components reach a specific degree of ageing.

  • Proposed maintenance framework

【Maintenance categories】

  • 考虑四种运维操作：
    • 故障更换（failure replacement）
    • 预防性更换（preventive replacement）
    • 大修（major repair）
    • 基本维修（basic maintenance）
