叶片多尺度物理建模：从微观损伤到宏观失效

Oct 02, 2025

风机叶片物理模型文献整理

参考文献：

Sørensen, B. F., Castro, O., Chen, X., & Branner, K. (2025). Fatigue of composites for wind turbines blades. In Fatigue in Composites (pp. 555–596). Elsevier. https://doi.org/10.1016/B978-0-12-821288-2.00013-8

Bangaru, A. K., Miao, X.-Y., Sørensen, B. F., & Chen, X. (2023). Prediction of crack growth at trailing edge bondlines of a wind turbine rotor blade for the assessment of remaining service life: 43rd Risø International Symposium on Materials Science. 43rd Risoe International Symposium on Materials Science, 1293. https://doi.org/10.1088/1757-899X/1293/1/012037

Miao, X. Y., & Chen, X. (2023). Structural transverse cracking mechanisms of trailing edge regions in composite wind turbine blades. Composite Structures, 308(116680). https://doi.org/10.1016/j.compstruct.2023.116680

Multiscale approach

  • 为什么需要使用多尺度模型来进行模拟

    • 损伤区域需要使用较细的网格，例如对于后缘的粘合层脱粘，需要网格尺寸在毫米级别来捕获裂缝的简短应力场；而全尺度的叶片模型长度在100m尺度；（从计算效率，计算精度角度出发）
    • 从建模的最终目的出发：如果只需要计算结构频率和计算挠度，一维梁模型就可以；而想要模拟损伤发展过程，子结构模型的精度仍然不足以进行捕获，而是需要从microscale尺度进行理解；“In general, we think that damage interaction and scale effects in fracture of composite structures are so complicated that they can only be understood on the microscale, i.e., by microscale experiments and micromechanical modelling”

  • A top-down approach to fatigue of composite structures. (由上至下，使用全局模型的位移作为子结构模型的边界条件来实现多尺度建模)

    • “The displacement of a (global) full-blade model with a relative coarse mesh could be used as input (boundary conditions) for a substructure model, i.e. a more refined model of a smaller part of the blade (e.g. an FE model with much smaller element sizes, where the damage evolution prediction is made) of a smaller blade part, analogous to the control of boundary conditions for substructure testing.”

  • “some parts (some of the elements) in the global FE model with damage should have their properties (constitutive laws) changed so that their behaviour replicates that of the damage simulated in the submodel” (全局模型中存在损坏的区域应该改变其属性/本构定律来和子结构模型保持一致)

  • “a ‘consistent’ approach should refer to energy, such that the work of an applied force becomes the same for the global model as for the substructure model.” (跨尺度模型保持一致的方法应该涉及能量，使得全局模型和子结构模型所施加的力做的功保持一致)

  • “the output from a finer length scale model should be formulated in a sufficiently simple form such that it can be applied in a model at the coarser length scale.” 微观模型的输出应当以简单的形式表达，以便应用于大尺度模型中；

Trailing edge bondlines

Background

  • 后缘存在至少以下两种损伤：
    • “Tunnelling cracks (cracking in the adhesive along its width direction)"：沿叶片宽度方向；”While tunnelling cracks alone do not cause failure, in some cases can lead to the formation of interfacial cracks which can eventually fail by buckling of the blade“ 该种损伤不会导致叶片失效，但可能引发interfacial cracks
    • ”interfacial debonding, i.e., cracking along the interface between laminate and adhesive layer (in the blade length direction“ (沿叶片长度方向)
  • 后缘开裂可能形成的原因：”The damage formed was due to a combination of several factors such as extreme loading conditions, complex geometries and imperfections coming from manufacturing processes.“ 极端负载，复杂外形，制造缺陷
  • ”Haselbach et al. [5] tested a 34 m long blade by four-point loads and found that the local buckling of the trailing edge led to the debonding of the joint. It was concluded that the edgewise loads are the primary loads driving the failure of the trailing edge.“ ：沿边荷载导致后缘开裂

Research Gaps

  • “However, the method did not considered the edgewise loading that could lead to local buckling of the trailing edge laminate which can lead to debonding.” 未考虑沿边荷载作用
  • ”However, boundary conditions considered in the numerical model were based on subcomponent testing and not the full-scale blade.“ 基于子部件测试而非全尺寸叶片

Research Purposes

  • “The purpose of this study is to demonstrate a methodology for predicting damage growth at trailing edge joint of the wind turbine rotor blade.” ： 预测后缘裂缝增长
    • 考虑作用在叶片上的应力/应变
    • 边界条件
    • 铺层厚度和刚度
    • 初始脱粘长度造成的损伤状态（”the current damage state captured by an initial debond length parameter.“）
  • 使用一个一维分析模型（输入：铺层厚度，初始裂缝长度，叶片的重量分布，弹性模量，复合材料粘结层的断裂力学属性）进行裂缝预测，并使用3D有限元模型进行验证；

Analytical Modelling

  • 叶片——悬臂梁：根部固定，叶尖自由端

    • 因此叶片自身重力将导致edgewise stress

  • Assumption:

    • “we assume a simplified cross-sectional area of the blade model wherein, the analysis of only load-carrying laminates located at spar caps (pressure side and suction side), leading and trailing edges, are taken into account.”

  • 考虑重力和局部屈曲

FE Modelling

  • Abaqus， C3D8R，solid element
  • A 5m prismatic beam model from a 17m long Vindeby blade
  • mesh size:
    • 25 * 22 * 6 mm for spar cap
    • 25 * 11 * 14 mm for sandwich panels
    • 25 * 4 * 0.8 mm for trailing edge debondlines
    • Mesh size is chosen based on mesh sensitivitu analysis.
  • The virtual crack closure technique (VCCT)
